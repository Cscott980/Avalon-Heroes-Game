Avalon Heroes
What is Avalon Heroes?
Avalon Heroes is a web-based classroom Action RPG where students battle hordes of enemies as unique fantasy heroes — and grow stronger by answering teacher-created quiz questions.
Combat is fast, visual, and engaging.
Progression is powered by learning.
Students fight.
Students level up.
Students answer questions.
Students unlock power.
Learning is no longer a pause in the game — it drives the game.
________________________________________
What Is the Goal of Avalon Heroes?
Avalon Heroes transforms review sessions into interactive adventures.
Its goal is simple:
•	Reinforce knowledge
•	Increase engagement
•	Make repetition exciting
Instead of passively answering questions on a screen, students actively fight to survive, defeat bosses, and protect their team — with academic knowledge directly tied to their success.
Review becomes an event.
________________________________________
What Makes Avalon Heroes Different?
Unlike traditional quiz platforms such as Kahoot! or Blooket, Avalon Heroes does not treat questions as interruptions.
In Avalon Heroes:
•	Quiz answers unlock abilities
•	Correct responses create safe zones in boss fights
•	Knowledge weakens enemies
•	Mistakes carry meaningful in-game consequences
The quiz is not an add-on.
It is part of the world.
Students also choose a class — warrior, mage, ranger, and more — giving them identity, strategy, and ownership over their learning experience.
It feels like a real game, not just a game-styled quiz.
________________________________________
How Can Schools Use Avalon Heroes?
Avalon Heroes is fully web-based. No installation required.
It is optimized for:
•	Chromebooks
•	Tablets
•	Classroom PCs
Supports:
•	Keyboard & mouse
•	Gamepad
•	Touch controls
Teachers create and manage their own quizzes.
Sessions can be launched during review lessons, test preparation, or reinforcement periods.
It is designed specifically for real classroom environments.
